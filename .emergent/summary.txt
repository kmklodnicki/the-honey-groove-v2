**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Synthesized from initial request and subsequent user directives):**
*   **Core App:** A full-stack application for vinyl collectors to track their collection, log spins, and interact socially.
*   **Branding & Pages:** A warm, honey-inspired theme. Includes a landing page, About Page, FAQ Page, and an official logo/favicon. Includes a public beta signup page.
*   **User System & Profile:** Invite-only user accounts with profiles featuring avatar, bio, setup, location, favorite genre, stats, and a following system.     *   **The Hive (Feed):** A central social feed for user posts.
    *   **Daily Prompt:** A daily community prompt with streak tracking and a shareable export card.

    *   **Collector Bingo:** A weekly 5x5 bingo card.
*   **Search & Discovery:**
    *   **Global Search:** Live-updating search for records, collectors, and posts.
    *   **Explore Page:** A discovery page with various sections.
    *   **Record Detail Page:** A dedicated page for each record showing Discogs data and community stats.
*   **Collection Management & Valuation:** Integrates Discogs market data for collection value.
*   **The Honeypot (Marketplace):** A marketplace with a configurable platform fee.
    *   **Mutual Hold Trades:** A mandatory trade system where both parties place a refundable hold, equal to the records' value, to ensure transaction safety. Includes a dispute resolution system managed via the admin panel.
*   **Admin & Safety:** A unified admin panel for managing signups, invites, social features, user reports, platform fees, and trade disputes. A comprehensive buyer protection system is in development.
*   **Analytics & Notifications:**
    *   Google Tag Manager and custom event tracking.
