**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Synthesized from initial request and user directives in this session):**
*   **Core App:** A full-stack application for vinyl collectors to track their collection, log spins, and interact socially.
*   **Branding & UI/UX:** A warm, honey-inspired theme with a clean, modern UI, amber color palette, and specific fonts.
*   **Key Features & Terminology:**
    1.  **Core Concepts:** The app is built around three user-item categories with specific terminology:
        *   **Collection:** Records the user owns (formerly Reality).
        *   **Dream Items:** A passive wishlist of desired records (formerly Dreaming, Wishlist).
        *   **Actively Seeking:** An active wantlist of records the user is ready to buy (formerly The Hunt, Wantlist).
    2.  **Upgrade to Reality Workflow:** A modal appears when a user moves a record from Actively Seeking to their Collection, allowing them to set the final condition and price.
    3.  **Reverse Flow & Bulk Actions:** Users can move items from their Collection back to Dream Items or Actively Seeking individually (with confirmation) or in bulk.
    4.  **Taste Match Social Engine:** A comprehensive social feature that:
        *   Calculates a Taste Match percentage score between users.
        *   Displays a Record Soulmates 🍯 badge for high matches.
        *   Features an In Common tab on profiles to view shared records and dream items.
        *   Calculates and displays the monetary value of shared Dream Items.
    5.  **My Kinda People Discovery:** A carousel on the homepage suggesting other users with a high Taste Match.
    6.  **Gated Daily Prompt:** Users must Buzz In (answer the daily prompt) before they can view other users' answers in a new carousel interface.
    7.  **Consistent UI Overlays:** Frosted-glass pills for Variant Name and Honeypot Rating are now consistently overlaid on album art across all feed and collection cards for immediate visibility.
    8.  **UI/Terminology Refinement:** Multiple sessions of refining UI text, button colors, and layout for brand consistency, culminating in the Collection / Dream Items / Actively Seeking terminology.
