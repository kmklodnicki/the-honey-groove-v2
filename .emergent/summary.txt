**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Synthesized from initial request and subsequent user directives):**
-   **Core App:** A full-stack application for vinyl collectors to track their collection, log spins, and interact socially.

-   **Collection Management:** Users can add records to their collection. A Discogs import feature is planned.
-   **The Honeypot (Marketplace):** A three-tab marketplace: Shop (buy/sell), Trade (record swaps), and ISO (browsing community wantlists). Renamed from Marketplace.

-   **Trade System:** A complete, multi-step trade flow with offers, shipping tracking, confirmation, and disputes. Trades can include a cash Sweetener (formerly boot).
-   **Payments:** Stripe Connect integration for sellers. Buy It Now and Make Offer functionality. A 4% application fee is taken on all sales and Sweetener payments.
-   **Direct Messaging (DM):** A private messaging system with an inbox, conversation threads, and context cards for DMs initiated from trades or wantlists.
-   **Notifications:** An in-app notification center for social and transactional events.
-   **FAQ Page:** A dedicated page explaining the app's features in the brand's voice.
