<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a web app called **The HoneyGroove**, a social platform for vinyl collectors.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Synthesized from initial request and subsequent user directives):**
*   **Core App:** A full-stack application for vinyl collectors to track their collection, log spins, and interact socially.
*   **Branding & Pages:** A warm, honey-inspired theme. Includes a landing page, About Page, FAQ Page, and an official logo/favicon.
*   **User System & Profile:** User accounts with profiles featuring avatar, bio, setup, location, favorite genre, stats, and a following system. Includes a permanent Founding Member badge for the first 500 users.
*   **Onboarding:** A mandatory 3-step onboarding flow for new users to build their initial collection and follow other collectors.
*   **Social & Engagement Features:**
    *   **The Hive (Feed):** A central social feed for user posts, supporting types like Now Spinning, New Haul, ISO, and a new free-form A Note.
    *   **Daily Prompt:** A daily community prompt with a 'buzz in' flow, streak tracking, and a shareable export card.
    *   **The Mood Board:** A weekly auto-generated 3x3 grid of a user's most-spun album covers, exportable and shareable to The Hive.
    *   **Collector Bingo:** A weekly 5x5 bingo card with tactile animations, real-time bingo detection, community stats on locked cards, and a compact preview on the Explore page that opens into a full-screen modal. Features both active and locked countdown timers.
*   **Search & Discovery:**
    *   **Global Search:** A global, live-updating search for records, collectors, and posts, accessible from the main navigation.
    *   **Explore Page:** A discovery page with Trending, Taste Match, Fresh Pressings, Most Wanted, and Near You sections.
    *   **Record Detail Page:** A dedicated page for each record showing Discogs data, market value, community ownership stats, and related Hive posts.
*   **Collection Management & Valuation:** Integrates Discogs market data for collection value, Hidden Gems, and pricing assistance. All album art is cached.
*   **The Honeypot (Marketplace):** A marketplace with a configurable platform fee (currently 6%) on all transactions.
*   **Admin & Safety:** Admin panel sections for managing social features and a new configurable platform fee. A user-driven report and block system for posts, listings, and profiles.

**User's preferred language**: English
